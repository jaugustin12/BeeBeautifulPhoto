BEHIND THE LENS


"I'm Bettynah, Dely! Owner of Bee Beautiful Photography. Currently residing in PG County Hyattsville, MD. I am a Lifestyle Portrait Photographer. My love for Photography started out shooting in nature as my hobby. Whenever i had some free time i would grab my camera, go on a hike and wandered out into nature. The beauty of nature is what made me fell in love with photography. Something about being surrounded with God creations capturing his work as they are flaws and all bought a sense of joy inside of me. When i decided to turn photography into my career i made a promise to myself to capture my clients essence and beauty as they are."
